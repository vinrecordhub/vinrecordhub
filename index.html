FAQ

Common questions.

Why is VINRecordHub more affordable? +
VINRecordHub is a vehicle history service that provides access to Carfax® & AutoCheck® reports through our platform. By optimizing our delivery infrastructure, we offer competitive pricing while delivering the same comprehensive vehicle data.
How fast will I receive my report? +
Reports are delivered to your email inbox within 60 seconds of payment confirmation. You'll receive it as a downloadable file you can keep forever.
Where do I find the VIN on my car? +
The VIN is a 17-character code found on: the dashboard (visible through the windshield on the driver's side), the driver's door jamb sticker, your vehicle title, or your insurance card.
What if the report has no records? +
Some vehicles have limited history — normal for older vehicles or cars with minimal reported incidents. If your report returns no data, contact us at support@vinrecordhub.com and we'll resolve it or issue a full refund.
Is my payment secure? +
Yes. All payments are processed through PayPal — we never store or see your card details. PayPal's encryption keeps your information completely safe.
