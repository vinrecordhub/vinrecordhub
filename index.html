Common Questions

Got questions?

Why is VINRecordHub more affordable than going direct? +
VINRecordHub is a vehicle history service that provides access to vehicle history reports through our platform. By aggregating demand and optimizing our delivery infrastructure, we're able to offer competitive pricing while delivering the same comprehensive vehicle history data you need.
How quickly will I receive my report? +
Reports are delivered to your email inbox within 60 seconds of your payment being confirmed. You'll receive a PDF that you can download and keep forever.
Where do I find the VIN on my car? +
The VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) is a 17-character code found on: the dashboard (visible through the windshield on the driver's side), the driver's door jamb sticker, your vehicle title, or your insurance card.
What if the report comes back with no records? +
Some vehicles have limited history in the database — this is normal for older vehicles or cars with minimal reported incidents. If your report returns no data, contact us at support@vinrecordhub.com and we'll work to resolve it or issue a refund.
Do report packs expire? +
No — your report credits never expire. Buy a 10-pack today and use them whenever you need them. Perfect for dealers and frequent car buyers.
Is my payment information secure? +
Yes. All payments are processed through PayPal — we never store or see your card or bank details. PayPal's industry-leading encryption and fraud protection keeps your information completely safe.
