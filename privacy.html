Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use VINRecordHub, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — to deliver your vehicle history report
  • VIN numbers — to generate your requested report
  • Payment information — processed and stored by PayPal, not by us
  • Usage data — basic analytics like pages visited and browser type

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver your vehicle history report to your email
  • Process and confirm your payment
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Send important service updates (not marketing, unless you opt in)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only with:

  • PayPal — to process your payment securely
  • Data providers — VIN numbers are shared to generate your report
  • Legal authorities — if required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. All payment processing is handled by PayPal using industry-standard encryption. We do not store credit card or bank account information on our servers.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to improve your experience on our site. These cookies do not store personal information and cannot be used to identify you personally.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, and to request its deletion. Contact us at support@vinrecordhub.com to make such a request.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at support@vinrecordhub.com.
