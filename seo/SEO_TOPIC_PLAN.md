## How to read this

VinRecordHub is a **pay-per-report** vehicle-history storefront (static HTML on Vercel).
Its one conversion goal is a VIN purchase on **`/checkout`**. This plan builds an organic
content layer that funnels high-intent searchers to that page.

### ⚖️ Trademark-safe wording rules (apply to every topic)
- ✅ Allowed: "Carfax **alternative**", "an alternative to Carfax", "compare your options",
  "affordable vehicle history report", "cheaper report option", factual price comparisons.
- ❌ Avoid: claiming VinRecordHub **is** Carfax/AutoCheck, is an **official partner/reseller**,
  or delivers "**the real Carfax**/**same exact data**"; fake guarantees; logo misuse.
- Use the ® symbol on first mention of a competitor name and a one-line disclaimer:
  *"Carfax® and AutoCheck® are trademarks of their respective owners. VinRecordHub is an
  independent service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by them."*

### Keyword clusters
1. **Alternative / competitor** (highest value): carfax alternative, cheaper than carfax, cheap carfax, autocheck alternative, carfax competitor
2. **Affordability**: cheap / affordable / low-cost vehicle (car) history report
3. **Core report**: vehicle history report, car history report, used car report
4. **VIN**: VIN check, VIN report, instant VIN report
5. **Report-type intent**: accident history, title check, salvage title check, odometer check
6. **Transaction**: buy vehicle history report
7. **Brand-informational**: carfax (cost / worth-it)

# Content strategy (summary)

- **Hub-and-spoke:** Topic 6 (`/vehicle-history-report`) is the **pillar**; report-type pages
  (15–18), VIN pages (11–13) and affordability pages (7–9) are spokes that link up to it; it
  links down to them. The **alternative cluster** (1–5, 20) is a parallel hub that converts
  brand/comparison searchers and funnels them to `/checkout`.
- **Every page ends in a VIN CTA** to `/checkout` — the single conversion goal.
- **One reusable template** (header/footer/CTA + per-page `<head>` + `FAQPage`/`Article` schema)
  keeps 20 pages consistent and cheap to ship. See `SEO_IMPLEMENTATION_CHECKLIST.md`.
- **Trademark discipline** is a publishing gate, not an afterthought — the brand-adjacent
  topics (3, 20, and the "alternative" framing in 1, 2, 4, 5) only ship with the disclaimer
  and comparison-only language above.
- **Measure** with Search Console + the existing GA/Supabase analytics; track VIN-CTA clicks
  per page and prune/expand based on ranking + conversion.
